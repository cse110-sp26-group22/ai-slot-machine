# Results Analysis

## Overview

Three AI-generated scripts (5, 22, 27) were evaluated based on the differences observed between Step 2 and Step 3 outputs, with a focus on UI improvements, visual engagement, graphics, and audio enhancements.

From this evaluation, scripts **5 and 22** were selected for further analysis, while **27** was excluded due to much less noticeable improvements.

---

## Comparison Chart

| Script | UI Changes (Step 2 → Step 3) | Visual & Graphics Quality | Audio Enhancements | Overall Impact | Decision |
|--------|------------------------------|----------------------------|--------------------|----------------|----------|
| 5      | Significant improvements in layout and interactivity | Strong visuals and engaging graphics | Noticeable audio improvements | High | Selected |
| 22     | Clear UI refinement and better structure | High-quality visuals and graphics | Improved and more engaging audio | High | Selected |
| 27     | Strong UI with good usability, but little to no changes | Visually compelling and dynamic | Good audio experience | Low | Not Selected |

---

## Conclusion

Script 27 initially appeared strong, especially in its starting version:
  - It had solid visuals and a good foundation
  - However, **the changes between Step 1 and Step 2 were minimal**
  - This lack of iteration reduced its value for deeper analysis
  
It is also worth noting that prominently in candidate 5 and in other candiates, there was a visual glitch where the slot would not stop splinning even upon output of a result. This should be amended in the next refinement stage.
  
Thus we decided to not proceed with this test.
